Burn Body Fat Efficiently With These Fruits

Published 2/11/2019

There is no denying that fat is an important part of our body composition as it plays crucial roles. The body burns stored fat to provide it with the energy needed for our day to day activities. However, serious health issues can arise when body fat is in excess and not being utilized as it should.

Modern advancements in science and research have led to the discovery of different methods to burn the excess body fat. As such, there are a lot of diet supplements and medications that have been developed to help the body rid of excess fat. Yes, most of them work but others pose even greater health risks. I believe that opting for natural methods such as physical activeness and eating fruits are better than any medical supplement in the market.

It is vital to watch what we eat as some foods provide large quantities of fat which can work against us if left just hanging around in our bodies. The fruits discussed below are an excellent way to burn the excess quantities of fat coupled up with numerous health benefits.

1. Pomegranates

Pomegranates are packed with antioxidants and polyphenols. The combination of both is an efficient means of helping the body rid of toxins that facilitate the formation of fat.

If you are struggling with reducing your appetite, then you should consider making pomegranates part of your daily diet. Research has revealed that they have a significant impact on lowering appetite.

According to a study by the University of California, the antioxidants present in pomegranates boost metabolism, the body’s process of converting food into energy. Undoubtedly, a glass of pomegranate juice is loaded with health benefits.

2. Apples

If by any chance you have read our article on the 7 Everyday Fruits to Keep the Doctor Away, then you know the profound health benefits that consuming apples have for you.

Apples have tremendous health benefits as they provide loads of vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, and high fiber content. But research has revealed on top of all these, apples have significant fat-burning properties. The high fiber content they offer has proven to be a key factor in reducing the accumulation of body fat.

The fiber is soluble and the antioxidants reduce the body’s cholesterol levels as well as promote metabolic activities. To that end, apples are an excellent way of restricting the formation of fat and burning the excess already taken in by body cells.

3. Grapefruits

Grapefruits are about 90% water and provide high levels of fiber on top of other essential vitamins and minerals. Moreover, they possess antioxidants which by know you know are excellent in burning body fat.

A study by the Journal of Medicinal Food showed that grapefruit lowers cholesterol levels and you guessed it, body fat. Participants in the study recorded a significant reduction in their belly fat. Well, a fruit that keeps you hydrated, keeping your cholesterol and body fat in check is definitely one you should make part of your daily diet.

4. Berries

On top of the tremendous contribution that berries have in helping your body reduce the risk of chronic and cardiovascular diseases as well as cancer development, they possess significant fat-burning properties.

Berries have a high content of antioxidants that play a crucial role in getting rid of body toxins. The toxins are linked to numerous health problems and weight gain as they facilitate the formation of body fat. Blueberries, strawberries, and raspberries have a high content of polyphenols that have been found to restrict fat content from forming in the body.

Health research has been undertaken at the University of Michigan as well as the Texas Woman’s University involving feeding rats with extracts from berries. All endeavors showed that berries do indeed help burn fat and reduce its formation by a huge percentage.

5. Tomatoes

We all eat tomatoes. But what you might not know is that they contain minerals and nutrients that take part in helping the body get rid of excess fat. They offer phytonutrient carotenoid which is attributed to reducing the risk of cardiovascular and chronic diseases. Moreover, it is the antioxidant that has been revealed to enable tomatoes to possess significant body fat burning properties.

6. Lemons

It is well known that lemons are excellent in detoxifying the body. But they also have tremendous health benefits such as protecting against anemia, promoting heart health, preventing the formation of kidney stones as well as cancer development. Lemons contain soluble fiber promoting metabolism and helping the body use up the stored fat. A glass of lemon juice is just right considering all the benefits it has for you.

7. Watermelons

Just like grapefruits, watermelons are made of about 90% water. What’s more, they are packed with vitamins and antioxidants which offer many health benefits on top of keeping the body hydrated.

Research by the Journal of Nutritional Biochemistry revealed that watermelons prevent the accumulation of fat in body cells. They also help the body remove toxins as well as improve metabolism. Surely, this is a fruit you should be consuming regularly.

8. Coconuts

Coconuts have been discovered to facilitate the rate of metabolism. This is attributed to the high manganese content which also lowers cholesterol levels and promotes bone health. They also contain medium-chain triglycerides (MCTs) which are a type of fat that is rapidly used by the body to provide energy. A study which involved people with obesity discovered that their waists reduced by nearly 3cm after consuming coconut oil for a few weeks.

All fruits are nutritious and have their notable health benefits. But when it comes to helping your body burn the excess fat that can lead to serious health risks, the fruits discussed above stand out. Be sure to be enjoying any of them and have a clean conscience regarding your health.
